Our Story

Our love story kicked off in January 2018, thanks to Tinder. Lucy, being the more organised of the two, messaged Ben first (If I had waited for him, we'd probably still be single). Bonding over memes and The Chase, we quickly realized we had something special: a shared sense of humour and a mutual appreciation for useless trivia.

Our first date in Leeds confirmed it—we didn’t find each other annoying, so we figured we’d keep hanging out. By the end of 2018, we took the plunge and moved in together. Spoiler: we still didn’t annoy each other (much).

In 2021, we levelled up and moved to Glasgow, and by March 2023, Ben decided it was time to pop the question. In true BulliMack fashion, things didn’t exactly go as planned. Picture this: drunk leprechauns, a pitch-dark canal, and a car crashing into a wall at a leisurely 5 mph. Confused? So were we. Despite this Ben got down on one knee (after an intense cardio session up 5 flights of stairs to the Air BnB), Lucy breathlessly said yes (insert joke about Ben taking Lucy's breath away etc) and here we are today welcoming you to our special day.
